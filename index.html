AntherOS was born out of a specific vision: to build a visually perfect, hyper-responsive workspace environment tailored directly for gamers and power users without the heavy background ecosystem baggage.

By pulling operational logic from downstream Pop!_OS distributions, AntherOS secures advanced hardware compatibility layers, immediate native GPU driver mapping, and stellar system security parameters right from initial system boot sequences.
